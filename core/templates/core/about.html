About Let’s Go Foraging

Beginner-friendly foraging walks rooted in safe plant identification, seasonal wild food and local ecology.

What the walks are about

Let’s Go Foraging offers guided walks for people who are curious about wild food, local plants and the changing seasons.

The walks are designed to be welcoming and accessible for beginners, with a focus on careful identification, responsible foraging and noticing what grows around us.

What you can expect

  • Beginner-friendly plant identification
  • Seasonal wild food tasting
  • Guidance on safe and responsible foraging
  • Local ecology, folklore and practical uses
  • A relaxed pace with space for questions

Safety and responsibility

Foraging should always be approached carefully. Walks focus on building confidence slowly, understanding lookalikes, respecting local habitats and only gathering where it is safe and appropriate to do so.
